Boulder Dash + 
by Apollo 8502
have 10 games on one C64 disk.
In the following order:
- B.Dash 1
- B.Dash '2'              -- different to BoulderDash02
- Professional
- Trip To Hell
- Heaven
- Wild Willy
- Rock Bottom
- Raskapje
- Tough Times
- Kwadruif

It is like BD1 engine, but with an extra command "0xFE" (Raster, a bit different to existing).
GDash-Identifier is "GDASHBD+".
